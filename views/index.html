About

Listing your books on the internet to share your reads should have been a simple.
  • List titles of the books and their authors and a picture
  • Maybe split them to shelves

But that simple tasks are very rare to find. Most of the website are over-complicated piece of slow software. with many privacy invasive trackers, bloated with javascript and has alot of confusing and distracting features.

So this is why Library exists, it should be:

  • Free software as in freedom
  • Simple
  • Fast
  • Doesn't track me or my friends
Enjoy using library and if you need to have your own instance for you and your friends you can deploy your instance from Github
